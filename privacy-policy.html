Your privacy matters. CancerFunding.net is committed to protecting your personal information. This policy explains what we collect, how we use it, and your rights — please read it before submitting any information.

1

Who We Are

CancerFunding.net is a website that helps individuals with life-threatening or chronic illnesses — including cancer — explore the option of selling their life insurance policy for a lump-sum cash payment through a process known as a life or viatical settlement.

This Privacy Policy governs the collection and use of personal information submitted through CancerFunding.net and any related communications with our team.

2

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly, as well as information gathered automatically when you use our site.

Information you provide:

  • Full name, email address, and phone number
  • Health and medical information (diagnosis, age, life expectancy) when voluntarily provided
  • Life insurance policy details (carrier, face value, policy type)
  • Contact preferences and any other details submitted through our forms or questionnaires

Information collected automatically:

  • IP address and general geographic location
  • Browser type, device type, and operating system
  • Pages visited, time spent on site, and referring URLs
  • Cookie data and similar tracking technologies (see Section 5)
3

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Evaluate your eligibility for a life or viatical settlement
  • Contact you regarding your inquiry via phone, email, or text
  • Connect you with licensed settlement providers or brokers
  • Improve our website content, user experience, and marketing
  • Comply with applicable legal and regulatory requirements
  • Prevent fraud and protect the security of our services

Sensitive health information: Any health or medical information you provide is used solely to determine eligibility and facilitate the settlement process. We do not sell your health information to data brokers or use it for unrelated marketing purposes.

4

How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information in the following limited circumstances:

  • Affiliated settlement providers: We may share your information with licensed life settlement brokers, providers, or investors to evaluate and process your inquiry.
  • Service providers: We use third-party tools (such as CRM platforms, email providers, and analytics services) that may process your data on our behalf under confidentiality agreements.
  • Legal compliance: We may disclose your information if required by law, regulation, court order, or to protect our legal rights.
  • Business transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction.
5

Cookies & Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to operate and improve our website. These may include:

  • Essential cookies: Required for the site to function properly
  • Analytics cookies: Help us understand how visitors interact with the site (e.g., Google Analytics)
  • Marketing cookies: Used to track the effectiveness of advertising campaigns

You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences. Note that disabling certain cookies may affect site functionality. We may use third-party pixels or tracking tags from advertising platforms (such as Google or Meta) to measure campaign performance.

6

Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this policy, unless a longer retention period is required by law. If you request that we delete your information and we are not legally required to retain it, we will do so within a reasonable timeframe.

7

Security of Your Information

We implement reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, or misuse. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We encourage you to avoid sharing sensitive information (such as full Social Security numbers or financial account details) through our web forms.

8

Your Rights & Choices

Depending on your state of residence, you may have the following rights:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal information we hold about you
  • Correction: Request that we correct inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request that we delete your personal information, subject to legal obligations
  • Opt-out: Opt out of marketing communications at any time by clicking "unsubscribe" in any email or contacting us directly
  • Do Not Call: Request removal from our phone outreach list

To exercise any of these rights, contact us using the information in Section 12. We will respond within 30 days.

California residents: You may have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), including the right to know, delete, and opt out of the sale of personal information. We do not sell personal information as defined by the CCPA.

9

Children's Privacy

CancerFunding.net is not directed at individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors. If you believe a minor has submitted information to us, please contact us and we will promptly delete it.

10

Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites. This Privacy Policy applies only to CancerFunding.net and does not govern the practices of any external sites. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any third-party sites you visit.

11

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or applicable law. When we do, we will revise the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of the site after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
