About us

Honest, licensed, and built for cancer patients.

We help cancer patients and their families decide whether a life settlement is the right move. Sometimes the answer is yes. Sometimes it isn’t. We tell you either way.

Who we are

Cancer Funding is a licensed life and viatical settlement service founded to give cancer patients an honest, plain-English path to selling their life insurance policies when they need cash. We work with licensed providers in 45 states. We don’t pressure. We don’t oversell. And when a settlement isn’t your best option, we tell you.

What makes us different

Most settlement companies are optimized to close deals. We’re optimized to help patients make the right decision, even when that decision isn’t us. We publish our “when we say no” case studies because we think trust is earned by showing your work.

“We told a patient last month not to sell. His accelerated death benefit rider would pay nearly as much, with less paperwork. He’d never have learned that from a sales call.”

Our specialists

Every patient works with a licensed life settlement specialist (not a call center). Our team includes former oncology social workers, licensed insurance professionals, and certified elder law paralegals. We screen, we train, and we hold ourselves accountable to a “patient first” standard.

How we’re paid

We never charge patients. Our compensation comes from the providers who purchase your policy, and only when a settlement closes successfully. This means we’re motivated to find the best fit for your situation, not to push you into anything.

Want proof of our licensing? Our state licenses, BBB accreditation, and HIPAA compliance documentation are available on request. Just contact us or call (561) 461-9628.

Where we operate

Licensed in 45 states. The exceptions and states with non-standard rules are listed on our state-by-state page. All work can be done remotely, with secure document upload and remote notarization where allowed.
