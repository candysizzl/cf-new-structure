How it works

From eligibility check to cash in your account

Three steps, no upfront cost, no obligation. Most patients see funds in 30-60 days from acceptance.

01

Check eligibility

A 30-second, 4-question form. We ask about your policy face value, policy type, and diagnosis. No medical records, no credit check. You see an estimated range immediately.

02

Free policy valuation

Licensed providers evaluate your policy. We request medical records (with your authorization) and underwriting documents from your insurer. Within 2-4 weeks, you receive written offers — no obligation to accept.

03

Accept & get funded

If you accept an offer, paperwork is signed (often remotely with notarization). Funds wire directly to your bank account within 30-60 days. State law gives you a rescission window after signing.
